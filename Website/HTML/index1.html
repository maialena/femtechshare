About FemTechShare

FemTech is Cal's first tech club for women of all majors. Join us this semester as we launch FemTechShare, an 8-week Web Design and Development course for people who have no experience with coding and want an 'in' but don't know where to start. You don't need to have years of past coding experience to be a programmer. In fact, we encourage you to start at square one. We invite artists, scientists, humanitarians, and techies to join our motly crew. We value what you have to bring to the table. Click here for the details about how to get involved
